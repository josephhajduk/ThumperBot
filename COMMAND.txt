help - Gives Help
setmain - Sets your main character
listchars - Lists all your characters
pm - Sends a private message,  the first argument should be the player name.  If there are spaces in their name you need to put the entire name in quotes
mygroups - Lists all the groups you are a member of
joingroup - Applies to join a group
pinggroup - Sends a ping to a group
xupgroup - Sends a xup to a group
